My Role

I work as Interaction Designer for the the Automotive Experience Design Team, which is a variegated team of designers and user researchers. My daily tasks lies behind two mainstreams: Product and Innovation. From the product side (customer or internal programs) I support with the creation of information architecture, UX specification, wireframes, interaction design, prototypes and Validation of new product features. From the innovation side I explore new topics and further concepts, such as Autonomous driving, Voice interfaces, multimodality and sound design.
