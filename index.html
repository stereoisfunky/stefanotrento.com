me

Ciao!
I'm Stefano Trento,
Interaction Designer.

I design products and services for people's needs, combining user experience, multimodal interaction and product design.

Born in Bassano del Grappa, Italy in May 1988. I own a BA in sound engineering - Padua, Italy - and a MSc in New Media Technology - Copenhagen, Denmark.

I am a curious person and I need to constantly learn something new. I look at the holistic picture of things, but at the same time I research for details and their connections.

I love to hear and tell stories, original or fake it does not matter. I can work alone, but I prefer to do things together with others. I am a shark of collaboration and I try to get involved in any challenging projects. No pain no gain.
On my free time I travel, I make and play music, I dance like a Power Ranger, I bike as much as I can and I watch old movies.

I am currently based in Berlin working as Interaction Designer at HERE Maps in the Automotive Experience Team. If you want to read more then here is my CV
